## ğŸ¯ The 7 Stages (Simple Explanation)

### A. Research (1-2 weeks)
**"What can I build?"**
- Check council rules for your land
- Get planning certificates
- Talk to a planner

### B. Plans & Reports (1-3 months)
**"Create detailed plans"**
- Hire architect/designer
- Get engineering reports
- Prepare all required documents

### C. Submit Application (1-3 days)
**"Send it to council"**
- Fill out forms
- Pay fees
- Lodge your DA

### D. Council Review (2-6 weeks)
**"Council checks everything"**
- Council reviews your plans
- Neighbors may be notified
- You may need to provide more info

### E. Decision (1 week)
**"Get approved or refused"**
- Council makes decision
- Review conditions
- Pay contributions if approved

### F. Building Approval (2-4 weeks)
**"Get permission to build"**
- Hire certifier
- Get Construction Certificate
- Final checks before building

### G. Construction (6-12+ months)
**"Build and move in"**
- Start building
- Inspections at key stages
- Get Occupation Certificate to move in
